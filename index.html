    We love the GULF OF PORTO, it is one of Corsica’s most famous landscapes. The pinnacles and ravines of the red granite Calanche soaring out of beautiful blue seas are framed behind by the jagged peaks of PAGLIA ORBA - the island’s second highest peak. They are best explored by foot or by sea and there are daily boat trips from Porto, which visits this UNESCO world heritage site.
